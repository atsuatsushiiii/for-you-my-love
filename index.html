A Devotion Bestowed Upon You Alone

My Dearest Love,
This may not be a very dramatic confession but this one came from my heart. Alam ko na napaka aga neto, at alam kong kakabalik lang natin matapos ang mahabang panahon, pero gusto ko lang maging totoo sa nararamdaman ko para sa’yo. Mahal pa rin kita, and nandito pa rin yung nararamdaman na ’to sa akin.
While I’m writing this, I couldn’t sleep. Ang dami pa ring tumatakbo sa isip ko, and I’m not even sure if I can explain everything the right way—but I’ll do my best to make it clear for you.
In those 3 years na nag-uusap tayo, I really regret not getting to know you more and not understanding you better. I should’ve manned up and been more open, pero bilang isang lalaking hindi pa talaga alam noon kung ano ang tunay na pagmamahal, I chose to stay inside my own box and just let things pass by. I kept everything to myself instead of taking the risk to express what I truly felt.
Looking back now, I realize how much I missed—not just the chance to be closer to you, but also the chance to grow as a person.
Alam mo, hanggang ngayon, may mga nagtatanong pa rin sa’kin kung paano kong nagawang manatiling consistent sa’yo sa loob ng tatlong taon. At mula noon hanggang ngayon, iisa pa rin ang sagot ko—dahil mahal kita. Dahil ikaw ang pinipili ko araw-araw.
Sa totoo lang, hindi naman mahirap manatili sa babaeng mahal mo. Kapag totoo ang nararamdaman mo, kusa mo iyong ginagawa, hindi ba?
Like my late beautiful nanay—my lola—used to say: “May mga pusong hindi napapagod magmahal, lalo na kapag iisang pangalan lang ang inuuwian.”
I know I should’ve been braver. I should’ve asked more, listened more, and made more effort to understand you instead of assuming things or staying silent. At that time, I thought I had all the time in the world, but now I realize that time doesn’t wait for anyone. I regret not making the most of the moments we had—even the small ones that could’ve meant something more.
It has been 5 years, 7 months, and 30 days since we separated as I write this letter, and I know that our minds are now more mature. And I hope that with this maturity, we can finally understand each other better without pressure or confusion.

And I want to change things this time, because I want you.
I WANT YOU.
I want to understand you, I want to know you more deeply, I want to protect you, I want to support you, I want to help you, and be there for you in ways I wasn’t before.
I want to learn you again—not just the version I knew before, but the person you are now. I want to grow with you, understand your thoughts, your silence, your happiness, and even your struggles.
I want to be someone you can rely on, someone who stays consistent, someone who doesn’t disappear when things get hard.
And like always I am still willing to spend the rest of my life with you. I want this because I want you, and I will spend my days working toward the life I see when I close my eyes and think of us.
I know this is a lot to take in pero gusto ko lang talaga maging totoo sa nararamdaman ko.
Whatever your feelings are, I will respect them completely. walang halong pressure... pero may halong expectation. Napaka sinungaling ko naman siguro kung sasabihin kong wala akong ineexpect sayo. Still whatever your answer is, I will accept it.
I’m just grateful that we were able to reconnect and talk again after all this time. Take as much time as you need, at sana mas maunawaan natin ang isa't isa nang mas maayos.

With love,

Kevin
